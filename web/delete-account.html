🍺 Beerer — Delete Your Account

Last updated: 20 March 2026 · Developer: Ondrej Hnyk

This page explains how to request the deletion of your Beerer account and associated data, as required by Google Play's data deletion policy.

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account directly from within the Beerer app:

  1. Open the Beerer app and sign in.
  2. Tap your profile avatar or go to Settings.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete Account".
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.
  5. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Note: You must be signed in to delete your account via the app. If you no longer have access to your account, use the email request method below.

Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can submit a deletion request by email:

  1. Send an email to ondrej.hnyk@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: "Beerer — Account Deletion Request".
  3. Include the email address associated with your Beerer account.
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently deleted:

Data type Action Retention after request
Firebase Authentication record (email, sign-in provider) Deleted Immediately on deletion
User profile (nickname, weight, age, gender, preferences) Deleted Within 30 days
FCM push notification token Deleted Within 30 days
Your personal pour records in sessions you participated in Deleted Within 30 days
Keg sessions you created (and all associated pours & joint accounts) Deleted Within 30 days

What Data May Be Retained

Anonymised session contributions: In sessions created by other users that you participated in, your pour records may be retained in anonymised form (the user ID is removed, the nickname shown becomes "Deleted User") so that the session statistics and cost summaries remain accurate for the other participants. This anonymised data contains no information that can identify you.

Data type Action Retention period
Anonymised pour volumes in other users' sessions Anonymised Until the session creator deletes the session

BAC estimates are never stored on any server — they are calculated on your device only and are not subject to retention or deletion requests.
